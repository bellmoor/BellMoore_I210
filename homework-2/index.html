The Martians Are Real and Fluffy

Written by Ollie Tabooger

New Species Discovered

New life was discovered on Mars just ten hours ago. NASA - despite serious budget cuts - was successful in creating an artificial intelligence mining rover to the planet. This rover - The Floofer Detector - found an undiscovered cave entrance on the surface. The Floofer Detector scanned the entrance and found an unexpected heat signal that it followed. What it did net was unprecedanted. The AI Rover went into the cave and took itself apart to fit into the 2 foot wide opening. It left any non-vital protective pieces outside the cave opening and entered. When it got to a wider opening in the dark space, it spun in circles, thouroughly dizzying the scientists wathching the streamed footogae. When the Floofer Detector stopped, there was something in the camera view that scientists had never seen and didn't know what t omake of it. They saw a Martian.

What Comes Next?

Obviously, there is only one possible conclusion for what comes next for our world. Floofy aliens are going to take over our biggest cities and the avengers will have to take them down. The fight will be close, lots of people will get really close to dying at least twice and think their mission has failed... but it wont and they dont. All will be fine, the Floofers will not destroy all life on Earth the same way they once did to Mars and live deep within the tectonic plates of Earth's crust. The Floofers never actually existed and this publication was all an April Fools joke to give people a laugh and get us lots of views.
